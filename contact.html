Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Office hours are 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

How do I apply for admission?

You can start the admission process by filling out our online inquiry form or contacting our admissions office directly.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes, we offer various financial aid options and scholarships. Please contact our financial aid office for more information.

Can I schedule a campus tour?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective families to tour our campus. Please use the contact form to schedule a visit.
